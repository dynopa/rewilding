Adjacency

Plants need to be near their dependencies and far from plants that are their same type but different species.
You can have lichen near other lichen but not near moss, basically.
Different plants have different needs, but all contribute the same in accordance with their type.

Time

  • Players have a set amount of time to accomplish what they want to do everyday, set by their suit's energy limits.
  • At the end of each day, players are then given time to set up what they need to print before resting for the next day.
  • Days are about 3 months apart.
  • Later, they can upgrade their suit to allow them to spend more time outside.

Printing

Each plant has a certain material amount that can be converted into biomats which are used to print tools.

Tools

  • Shovel: To dig holes
  • Sprayer: to water
  • Sucker: to get the Essence of the plants and then spray it (may be the same as ^)
