Rewilding Narrative

A narrative about rewilding.

World Setting

  • You play someone who works for a conservation program trying to re-establish life on a dead Earth.
  • The common working joe has taken to the skies, now living in one of the many space stations in orbit of Earth.
  • The corporate elite still live in massive arcologies, domed complexes that cover the major cities of old, across the planet, living off of artificial air and food.
  • Only now is rewilding, the concept of giving an area back to nature, economically feasible for these elite, so you have been hired to work the trenchs, so to speak.

Start

The game starts with a black screen with the audio from the player's experience of their rocketship landing on Earth.

Then the player goes out into the world and enters their DOME.

Structure

Events that happen on a day-by-day basis. Events can be based on:
  • Master Schedule: Happen on the same day no matter what
  • Random: Happen randomly
  • Daily % increase: Happens randomly, but with increased percentage for happenign after events
  • Result: Happens as a result of another event
Events have the following traits:
  • Description
  • Effect: what they do
  • Type: what type of the above bases

Local Setting

You're on an island in the Atlantic Ocean.
The island you are on was someone's home before they had to abandon it/died bc of... climate change.
